Privacy Policy

Effective date: 25 March 2026  ·  Last updated: 25 March 2026
Applies to: 1 Million Beers Android app and website (1millionbeersapp.com)

KDC Holdings (Pty) Ltd, trading as 1 Million Beers ("we", "us", "our"), is committed to protecting your personal information in accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act 4 of 2013 (POPIA) of South Africa. This policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, how we store it, and your rights regarding that information.

By registering for or using the 1 Million Beers app, you confirm that you have read and understood this policy and that you are at least 18 years of age.

1. Who We Are

Legal entity: KDC Holdings (Pty) Ltd
Trading as: 1 Million Beers
Website: 1millionbeersapp.com
Contact / Information Officer: support@1millionbeersapp.com
Country of incorporation: Republic of South Africa

2. Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to operate the app:

Account information

  • First name, last name, and optional display name
  • Email address
  • Date of birth (used solely to verify you are 18 or older — stored as an immutable legal consent record)
  • Profile photo (optional)
  • Google account ID (if you sign in with Google)

Content you create

  • Beer submission photos and captions you upload
  • Comments and likes on group activity feeds
  • Group names and group profile images

Device and technical information

  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) device token — used only to send you push notifications
  • App crash reports and diagnostic data via Firebase Crashlytics
  • Anonymous usage analytics via Firebase Analytics (e.g. screens viewed, features used)

Legal consent record

  • At registration we store an immutable record of your confirmed date of birth, the version of these Terms you accepted, your consent timestamp, and the platform (Android/iOS). This record cannot be deleted as it forms part of our age-verification audit trail.

3. Why We Collect Your Information (Purpose)

  • Age verification — to enforce our 18+ requirement as required by South African law
  • Account management — to create and manage your account and profile
  • App functionality — to display group feeds, leaderboards, achievements, and notifications
  • Safety and moderation — submitted photos are reviewed (and may be passed to an AI content moderation service) to ensure they meet community guidelines
  • Push notifications — to notify you of activity in your groups
  • Service improvement — anonymous crash reports and analytics help us fix bugs and improve the app
  • Legal compliance — to comply with POPIA and any applicable South African law

4. How We Store Your Information

Your personal data is stored securely on Google Cloud Platform (GCP) servers located in the Johannesburg, South Africa region (africa-south1). Our database is encrypted at rest and in transit.

Firebase services (Authentication, Analytics, Crashlytics, Cloud Messaging) are operated by Google and may process data on servers outside South Africa. Google acts as an operator processing personal information on our behalf under data processing agreements that meet applicable legal requirements.

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active. See section 7 for your right to request deletion.

5. Third Parties We Share Data With

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with the following service providers:

  • Google Firebase — Authentication, push notifications (FCM), crash reporting (Crashlytics), anonymous usage analytics
  • Google Cloud Platform — Secure database and API hosting
  • OpenAI — Submitted beer photos may be sent to OpenAI's API for automated content moderation. Photos are not stored by OpenAI beyond the moderation request.

Each of these providers maintains their own privacy policies and security standards. We have data processing agreements in place with each provider.

6. Analytics and Tracking

We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous, aggregated data about how the app is used (e.g. which features are most popular, how often the app is opened). This data cannot be used to identify you personally. No advertising identifiers are collected at this time.

If we add advertising to the app in future, we will update this policy and obtain any required additional consent before enabling ad personalisation.

7. Your Rights Under POPIA

As a data subject under POPIA you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction — request that inaccurate or incomplete information be corrected (you can update most details in the app under Profile settings)
  • Deletion — request deletion of your account and associated personal data. You can do this directly in the app (Settings → Delete Account) or by emailing us. Note: the immutable legal consent record (age verification) will be anonymised rather than deleted.
  • Objection — object to the processing of your personal information
  • Complaint — lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator of South Africa at inforegulator.org.za if you believe your rights have been violated

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@1millionbeersapp.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Children and Age Restriction

1 Million Beers is strictly for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If we become aware that a user is under 18, their account will be suspended and their data deleted. If you believe an underage person has registered, please contact us immediately at support@1millionbeersapp.com.

9. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. These include encrypted storage, encrypted transmission (HTTPS/TLS), access controls, and regular security reviews.

No system is 100% secure. If you suspect your account has been compromised, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via a notification in the app or by email. Your continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
